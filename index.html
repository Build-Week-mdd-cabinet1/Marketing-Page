Usefulness of Marijuana as a Medicine

Medical marijuana is a relatively new term. While people have known about it's medical qualities for thousands of years, in the last hundred years or so it has been deemed by governments as a drug with absolutely no positive or medical benefits. It is now coming back into common knowledge that medical marijuana can be used to change lives. It is remarkable as a medicine to treat nausea, insomnia, aches and pains, and its effects can even help relieve serious or life threatening conditions such as seizures. Another unique and exciting aspect of marijuana as a medicine is that each strain can have very different medical properties. A certain strain can help an insomniac find sleep, while another can help a user with chronic pain get energized and pain-free as they start their day. This range of medical relief all comes from a plant bred for thousands of years, as opposed to new drugs tested on a small group for a few years before hitting the market to be sold at a huge markup.
